Rose Darling

There was a rose.

And a rosebush where
A whippoorwhill sang:
"Poor Will, whipped!
"Poor, poor Will..."

And where there's a Will,
There's always some whey
Whey-faced, disgraced:
Wheyboy, whey:
Is it your churn yet?

Across the rosedark sea
Waveboy, wave
Wave for they are a-comin'

And coming near they clatter
Like skull-braids tumbling
Down the temple steps

Rosedark sea, oh now you see
It is not to be!
Cause it's all over
A four-leafed clover
My lovely lassie, Baby Grue

Grue: What if it were true?
All the same until today
And all the blame tomorrow?

All are game until today
And all are grim tomorrow
What fixed point is the end?
A rose of constant sorrow

Sam I am, Sam I am
Would I have to borrow?
Soldier boy, soldier toy
A rose of constant sorrow

Back to three...
Leave us be!
To sail the rosedark sea

And chant our Hail Mary
full of Greece
along the rosedark sea

Where in your ship
Paul is with thee
upon the rosedark sea

Once reaching shore
Blossomed are thou
Amongst all of we men

And blossomed is the fruit
Of the wounded Jesus,
Hanging o'er the rosedark sea

And now we plunge us
Down too deep
Beneath the rosedark sea

We pray onboard
Our souls do weep
They rose in constant sorrow

And out on the sea
Where Odysseus be
Rose waves of constant sorrow

There Captain Drake
No doubt a fake
Fransican robes did borrow

And Admiral Nelson
One-eyed past Corsica
Was shot dead at Trafalgar

Our heroes wail
They rant and rail:
"What a fate! Lost at sea,
"'Neath waves of constant sorrow!"
